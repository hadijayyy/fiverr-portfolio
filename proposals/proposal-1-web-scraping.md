**Here's how I work:**

3. **Validate** — You'll receive a sample of the extracted data to review before full delivery.
4. **Deliver** — Final dataset in your preferred format (CSV, JSON, Excel, database, API-ready).

- Reliable extraction that handles pagination, logins, and dynamic content
