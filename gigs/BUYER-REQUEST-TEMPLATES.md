## Tips for Sending Buyer Request Proposals

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| **Customize the first sentence** | Fiverr buyers can tell when you've copy-pasted. Personalize the `{project_details}` section. |
| **Keep it concise** | Fiverr previews show only the first ~100 characters. Make the opening line count. |
| **Attach a sample file** | If possible, attach a small CSV or screenshot of past work. Buyers love seeing real output. |
| **Be specific** | Reference details from their request. "I see you need real estate data from Zillow" is better than "I can scrape websites." |
| **Include a call to action** | Always end with a clear next step: "Send me the URLs and I'll provide a quote." |
| **Respond quickly** | Buyer requests are first-come. Respond within minutes of new postings. |
| **Check your gig** | Make sure your gig is active and the pricing matches what you quote in the proposal. |
